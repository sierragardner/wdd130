Overview

Purpose

My fiance and I want to run a car detailing business together. He will be in charge of the operations, while I will be behind the scenes marketing for the business mainly through a website. The purpose of this website is to show customers the different services that are offered and how much they will cost. Additionally, customers would be able to schedule appointments through the website and find an FAQ page, all with the purpose of cutting out the hassle of having to call the business directly. The biggest issue that we have run into as a business is trying to keep appointments in line, so this will be a great solution to the question of how to make this run smoothly and with ease for both us and our customers.

Audience

Those who want a clean car

Content

Home page

Why will people visit this site? This is the homepage that will grab attention of the users when first visiting it. We want people to see at first glance what services are offered and how we can best service them at BM Detailing. This homepage will have an overview of the best services available, what the prices points are, where they can set appointments, and be able to answer any questions that they may have for us. As of now, this business is only a mobile detailing service, so we want to be sure that customers are aware of that before they book an appointment with us.

Images for the Home page

Our Services

What questions will this page answer? This page is going to go over what each package detail will include. There are 3 options for the packages(Basic, Express, and Deluxe), and there will be options for add-on. Additionally, if they only want one thing done, such as only their seats shampooed, there are options for that. Prices will be on a case by case basis, so customers will have to request an appointment. Essentially, this page is going to be the meat of the whole website.

Images for the Page 2

Book an Appointment

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? This is where all of our customers will be able to set up an appointment to get their car detailed. The pricing of detailing will always vary depending on the size of vehicle, what exact services you desire, where you live, etc. This is where we, as a business, will be able to help a customer find the best fit package for their car and answer any questions that the customer may have. Through this appointment page, we will be able to complete the entire purpose of the site: connecting customers with our business to get their car to the level of perfection they desire.
